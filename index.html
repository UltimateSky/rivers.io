About

Just a sneak peek into my life.



Hey! My name is Charles and I'm currently a student majoring in Computer Science at the beautiful Georgia Southern University. Currently I am a full-time freelancer, an Ultimate Frisbee player at Georgia Southern and according to my friends, a part-time comedian (but only when I’m not trying).

I consider myself to be an individual of style and preference, as is translated through not just the way I present myself, but also through my work. I am inspired by all aspects of modern design; I believe there is elegance in both simplicity and boldness. Incorporating modernism into my designs and developments while also staying current with the ever-changing technological world is something truly distinguishable from the more traditional varieties.

Resume

Here are some of my previous work experiences.

  • 2008-2011

    Northside High School Class of '11

    I graduated from Northside High School in 2011. While at NHS, I was in the PLTW engineering program, and the Website Administrator for our school website (Dreamweaver). I was a part of the school robotics team, where we competed at the F.I.R.S.T., B.E.S.T. and G.R.I.T.S. robotics competitions; all robots were programmed using Java. I was also a member of the yearbook club (InDesign) during my senior year.

  • 2011-2012

    Georgia Southern University Hall Council

    From August 2011 through May 2012 I was an officer of the Southern Pines Hall Council at Georgia Southern University. More specifically, I was the treasurer. I assisted with creating and executing community events and managing the budgets for the semester. I also created all graphic designs (Photoshop) and handled the social media for the Hall Council.

  • 2012-2013

    Georgia Residence Hall Organization Public Relations Chair

    During my time as Treasurer for Hall Council, I was hand-picked to be the Public Relations Chair for the Georgia Residence Hall Organization annual conference. I was given the responsibility of creating the conference manual (InDesign) and designing all graphics for the conference (Photoshop/Illustrator).

  • 2012-2013

    Georgia Southern University Community Leader

    As a Georgia Southern Community Leader, some of my responsibilities included effectively working with my Hall team to effectively manage the residents of the hall. I was also responsible for hosting events, making flyers, team coordination, as well as being in charge of Hall Council and being a student liaison for residents.

  • 2015-2016

    Georgia Southern University Community Assistant

    After working as a Community Leader, I started working as a Community Assistant for Georgia Southern. Community Assistants were responsible for assisting administrators with whatever necessary, which included sending emails, creating spreadsheets, making documents and copies, and making flyers. I was also required to use both the Google Doc suite and Microsoft Office to effectively sync documents and share them with whoever needed them.

  • January 2016 - May 2016

    Web Developer

    For my Software Engineering class, we were required to take on a full project for a legitimate client and complete it to their specifications. For this project, my team and I created Solubuddy as seen above which is an analytical chemistry studing web application that is scaleable to all platforms including tablets and phones. Solubuddy was built using HTML, CSS3, and Javascript, with a PHP database backend as well. We also used Github to collaborate together.
